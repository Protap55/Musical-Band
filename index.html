    THE BAND

    "Protap – Rocking the Revolution!"

    Protap is more than just a rock band—it’s a movement, a soundwave of rebellion, and a force that shakes the ground beneath. Born from the passion for music and the fire of revolution, we blend powerful riffs, electrifying vocals, and heart-thumping beats to create anthems that resonate with the soul. From underground gigs to larger stages, Protap is here to break boundaries, ignite emotions, and amplify the voice of a new generation. Our music is raw, untamed, and fearless—just like the spirit of rock itself. Join us on this journey, feel the energy, and let’s Rock the Revolution!
